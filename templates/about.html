FAQ

Everything you need to know about the product and billing.

What is Gold Privilege membership?

+

Gold Privilege is an exclusive membership network that provides access to premium lifestyle experiences, entertainment venues, healthcare facilities, and hospitality services with special discounts and privileges.

How do I become a member?

+

Simply register on our platform, choose a membership plan that suits your needs, and complete the payment process. You'll get instant access to all member benefits and exclusive privileges.

What are the membership benefits?

+

Members enjoy exclusive discounts at partner venues, priority bookings, complimentary upgrades, cashback rewards, and access to premium events and experiences across multiple lifestyle categories.

Can I cancel my membership?

+

Yes, you can cancel your membership at any time from your account dashboard. Your benefits will remain active until the end of your current billing cycle.

How do I use my membership benefits?

+

Simply present your digital membership card or booking reference at any partner venue. You can also book directly through our platform to automatically apply your member discounts and benefits.

Are there different membership tiers?

+

Yes, we offer multiple membership tiers including Silver, Gold, and Platinum, each with increasing levels of benefits, discounts, and exclusive access to premium services.
