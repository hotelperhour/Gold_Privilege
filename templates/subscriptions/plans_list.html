FAQ

Everything you need to know about subscriptions, payments, and security.

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit/debit cards (Visa, Mastercard, Verve), bank transfers, and USSD payments through our secure payment gateway, Paystack. All transactions are encrypted and secure.

Is my payment information secure?

+

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and partner with Paystack, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. We never store your card details on our servers – all payment data is handled directly by our secure payment gateway.

How does the subscription billing work?

+

Your subscription is billed at the start of each billing period (monthly, quarterly, or annually according to your chosen plan). You’ll receive a receipt via email after each successful payment. You can manage or cancel your subscription anytime from your dashboard.

Can I get a refund if I cancel early?

+

We do not offer refunds for partial billing periods. If you cancel, you will continue to have access until the end of your current paid period. No further charges will be made after cancellation.

What happens if my payment fails?

+

If a recurring payment fails, we will notify you by email and attempt the payment again after a few days. Your subscription will remain active during this grace period. After repeated failures, access may be suspended until the payment is successfully processed.

How do I update my payment method?

+

You can update your payment method at any time from your account dashboard under "Payment Methods". Changes will apply to future billing cycles.
